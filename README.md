# PC-Idol
This allows you to either show of your PC online but you don't want to show your desktop or just for fun.

-How to install?
To Install PC-Idol, download [PC-Idol](https://github.com/tamzzinity/PC-Idol).

-How To Use?
(Windows 10 and 11) Right click on PC-Idol and click open with. Next select your browser you want and the HTML file should run on the browser showing a colourful gradient changing colours. To full screen click F11.

(Linux) Double click PC-Idol.html and it should open up in your browser. Then click F11 on your keyboard to make it full screen.
